Job Description for SAP

Responsibilities:

  • Define and design use cases, workflowsand storyboards describing how the user would interact with easy-to-use, small footprint apps.
  • Identify all elements of interaction and navigation, and lay them out within a screen so they direct the user without confusion or error.
  • Create consumer grade usable designs using wireframes and prototypes.
  • Create exciting information visualizations enabling the user to instantly understand the data in the right context, spot patterns, trends and relations in a self-service manner.
  • Participate in, and integrate feedback from user research.
  • Present your designs in team design reviews as well as to the extended group of internal stakeholders.
  • Participate in iterative design, testing and development sprint cycles to refine the overall product.
  • Collaborate with designers, solution managers, developers, testing team and possibly external agencies to ensure that the designs are implemented as effective user experiences.
  • Contribute to the successful launch of emerging solutions on multiple platforms.

Requirements:

  • Expertise with Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator
  • Pursuing degree in Interaction Design, Communication Design, Human Computer Interaction, Human Factors or other related user experience design disciplines.
  • Well developed skills in designing compelling user experiences, wire framing, sketching (story boarding, scenarios, rough layouts, mock-ups), and user interaction design are critical.
  • Understand the process of defining, designing and delivering consumer applications.
  • Knowledgeable about the user interface capabilities and standards of leading application platforms.
  • Knowledgeable about the technologies underlying software interfaces, and able to absorb quickly what you don’t already know. Good at solving interface and interaction design problems, offering not just one but a variety of viable solutions.
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to effectively engage and convey ideas to cross-functional technical and non-technical teams
  • Passion for end-user focused software applications design and development with an appreciation and empathy for the entire software design process including strategy, development, and production rollout.
