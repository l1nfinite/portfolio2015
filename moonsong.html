Brief

This was an individual based project for a upper level film course. The only criteria we had to fulfill was to use somehow use chroma keying in our final film. I chose to do film a cover of my friend doing "The Moon Song" as seem in the Film Her. I personally loved both the film and song, and so I wanted to see what I could create.
